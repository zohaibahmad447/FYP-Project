How Quick Care Works

From finding a doctor to receiving your prescription — here is the full journey on our platform.

1. Create account

Register as a patient or doctor. Doctors complete verification before accepting appointments.

2. Book & pay

Choose a specialist, pick a slot, and complete secure payment. Doctor approves your request.

3. Consult

Join a video call or in-person visit, chat with your doctor, and receive a digital prescription.
